MEET THE TEAM

TEAM DIRECTORS

Our team directors created Rohingya Eskul with a shared commitment to the Rohingya community; and to amplifying the voices of the young generation of Rohingya leaders. Together, our co-directors maintain the running of Rohingya Eskul through team coordination, project management, and most importantly, by shaping a vision with youth of a more equitable and just future for the Rohingya diaspora.
