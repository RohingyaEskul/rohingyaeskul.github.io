Our Policies

Social Media Policies

Terminology
Content Provider - The individual who initially shared a content piece (in any format) with Rohingya Eskul for the purpose of sharing it on Rohingya Eskul’s social media. Content is by default shared across any or all of Rohingya Eskul’s social media platforms, unless otherwise requested by the content provider.
Content Outreach Managers - The individual members of Rohingya Eskul that are in direct communication with content providers, and will have access to their contact details.

1.1 Crediting Content
We credit content providers by sharing their first name or a pseudonym with their consent.
We credit content providers by sharing the country the picture was taken in with their consent. We take into consideration if the country is one that the content provider is formally recognised in. We do not include the country of authorship if we feel the content could lead to backlash towards the community.

1.2 Rectifying Information
If at any point the content provider (or other parties visible in or clearly associated with the content) asks for information to be rectified, we commit to making those changes.

1.3 Requests for Removal
We do not share pictures that include the faces of individuals who are in countries where they are not formally recognised (including Myanmar).
We may use pictures of individuals who are formally recognised refugees in a host country, on the conditions that they (a) are over 18 years of age and (b) have given their consent.

1.4 Sharing Pictures of Individuals
We do not share pictures that include the faces of individuals who are in countries where they are not formally recognised (including Myanmar).
We may use pictures of individuals who are formally recognised refugees in a host country, on the conditions that they (a) are over 18 years of age and (b) have given their consent.

1.5 Sharing Personal Details of Content Providers
We do not actively store or maintain a database of information on content providers.
Content Outreach Managers at Rohingya Eskul are in communication with content providers, and will not share contact details or further elaborate on personal information with other team members of Rohingya Eskul or the public. Any further interaction is up to the discretion of the content provider.

Social Responsibility Policies

2.1 Accountability for Content
The opinions expressed in blog articles and social media posts reflect the views of content providers and/or individual members of the Rohingya Eskul team. Rohingya Eskul supports our members and wider community in using our platforms to share their stories, views and opinions.
We encourage interested parties to contact us via email and engage with us in discussion over any concerns regarding the published content. We are not responsible or liable for the accuracy or impact of the published content, or for any damage or loss to be caused by and in connection to it.

2.2 Sharing Contacts from our Network
As part of a network of Rohingya organisations we understand that we may be seen as a bridging point of contact for those looking to work with this community. While we intend to act on this role for the benefit of Rohingya organisations, the safety and privacy of individuals is our primary priority.
We are able to direct interested parties to Rohingya entities that we share a network with. For people or organisations that are looking for individual contacts for a specific purpose, we can also share these clearly outlined requests along with your contact details on our private communication channels, so that individuals are able to reach out to you directly.

Website Accessibility Statement

3.0 Accessibility Statement
Rohingyaeskul.org strives to ensure that its services are accessible to people with disabilities. Rohingyaeskul.org has invested resources to help ensure that its website is made easier to use and more accessible for people with disabilities, with the strong belief that every person has the right to live with dignity, equality, comfort and independence.

Rohingyaeskul.org makes available the Free Website Accessibility Widget that is powered by a dedicated accessibility server. The software allows Rohingyaeskul.org to improve its compliance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1).

The Rohingyaeskul.org accessibility menu can be enabled by clicking the accessibility menu icon that appears on the corner of the page. After triggering the accessibility menu, please wait a moment for the accessibility menu to load in its entirety.

Rohingyaeskul.org continues its efforts to constantly improve the accessibility of its site and services in the belief that it is our collective moral obligation to allow seamless, accessible and unhindered use also for those of us with disabilities.

Despite our efforts to make all pages and content on Rohingyaeskul.org fully accessible, some content may not have yet been fully adapted to the strictest accessibility standards. This may be a result of not having found or identified the most appropriate technological solution.

If you are experiencing difficulty with any content on Rohingyaeskul.org or require assistance with any part of our site, please contact us and we will do our best to assist.

If you wish to report an accessibility issue, have any questions or need assistance, please contact Rohingyaeskul.org at: hallo@rohingyaeskul.org
